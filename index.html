City

The City Cluster is the heart of Polokwane, and the commercial hub of the Limpopo Province. It is home to a number of businesses, shopping centers, restaurants, and entertainment venues. Visitors to the City Cluster can expect a vibrant and bustling atmosphere, with plenty to see and do. Some popular attractions in the City Cluster include the Polokwane Game Reserve, the Peter Mokaba Stadium, and the Meropa Casino and Entertainment World.

Township

The Township Cluster is the cultural soul of Polokwane, and home to some of the city's most vibrant communities. Visitors to the Township Cluster can expect a warm and welcoming atmosphere, and a chance to experience the rich cultural heritage of the region. Some popular attractions in the Township Cluster include the Makgofe Village Market, the Turfloop Cultural Village, and the Madi a Thavha Mountain Lodge.

Visit Our Townships

Village

The Village Cluster is where visitors can experience the tranquil beauty of rural life in Polokwane. Nestled amongst rolling hills and scenic landscapes, the Village Cluster is home to a number of charming small towns and villages. Visitors to the Village Cluster can expect a slower pace of life, and a chance to unwind and relax in peaceful surroundings. Some popular attractions in the Village Cluster include the Haenertsburg Village Market
